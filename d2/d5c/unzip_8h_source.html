275 /* Logic for case of small memory, length of EOL > 1: if OUTBUFSIZ == 2048,
276  * OUTBUFSIZ>>1 == 1024 and OUTBUFSIZ>>7 == 16; therefore rawbuf is 1008 bytes
277  * and transbuf 1040 bytes. Have room for 32 extra EOL chars; 1008/32 == 31.5
278  * chars/line, smaller than estimated 35-70 characters per line for C source
279  * and normal text. Hence difference is sufficient for most "average" files.
280  * (Argument scales for larger OUTBUFSIZ.)
281  */
